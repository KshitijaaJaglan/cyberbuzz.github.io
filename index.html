About Cyber Buzz


CyberBuzz is the prestigious tech symposium hosted annually by Amity International School Sector 43, Gurugram. From 4 events to a dozen this event has grown to be a learning experience for techies across Delhi NCR, enhancing their creativity in the field.
It is not everyday that Cyber enthusiasts get a Call To Arms. Cyber Buzz 2018 will not be just a test of knowledge, but also of skills in an Open Battlefield.
With 14 events covering the wide domain of computers and technology, CyberBuzz XIV will be truly test of the capability and the tech quotient of the participants.
Create a world, master another. Capture reality, tweak it later. Create Art, make it functional. From instructing computers to making the best ones, the events leave no aspect of Cyber age untouched.
